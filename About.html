What is LyCo CpE all about?

Founded last A.Y 2010-2011, LyCo – CpE is an organization of Computer Engineering students at LPU – L. The main goals of the organization is to share knowledge in information towards fellow CpE’s and help people through community extensions, seminars and other activities.


Mission

“Promote the advancement towards higher intensity of learning by establishing a sense of awareness and involvement among its members and thus, guide them towards professional competency.”


Vision

“The Lyceans’ Coalition of Computer Engineers envisions itself as the leading student organization driven by unity and camaraderie, forcing elite professional out of its members.”
